## ðŸŽ¯ Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Trust Through Quality**
- Consistent spacing (8px grid system)
- Professional color palette (navy/blue conveys stability)
- Serif headings + sans body = business-seriousness + readability
- No gradients or "creative" effects - clean, honest design

### 2. **Clear Hierarchy**
- Large, bold headlines (Merriweather serif)
- Readable body text (Inter sans-serif, 16px)
- Ample white space
- Logical content flow

### 3. **Trust Signals Everywhere**
- Client logos (topbar, hero, testimonials)
- Metrics (credibility strip, case studies)
- Badges (ISO, GDPR, Awards)
- Testimonials with photos + companies
- Team profiles with credentials

### 4. **Conversion-Focused CTAs**
- Action verbs: "Request a Quote", "Get Estimate", "Start Project"
- High contrast (mid-blue buttons on light backgrounds)
- One primary CTA per section
- Hover effects: lift + shadow

### 5. **Business-Appropriate Motion**
- Subtle fade/slide on reveal (200-400ms)
- Button hover: lift + darker shade
- Image hover: scale 1.05
- No excessive animation - professional restraint
